What Is a Percentage?

A percentage represents a number as a fraction of 100. It is commonly used in finance, statistics, shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam grading, banking interest rates and everyday budgeting decisions.

How To Calculate Percentages

Multiply the number by the percentage divided by 100. Example: 20% of 150 = (20 ÷ 100) × 150 = 30

How To Calculate Percentage Increase

(New − Original) ÷ Original × 100

How To Calculate Percentage Decrease

(Original − New) ÷ Original × 100
