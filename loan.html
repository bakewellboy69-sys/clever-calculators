How Loan Payments Are Calculated

Loan payments are calculated using a standard amortization formula that considers the loan amount, interest rate and loan term.

Loan Payment Formula

Monthly Payment = P × [ r(1+r)^n ] ÷ [ (1+r)^n − 1 ]

Where P is the loan amount, r is the monthly interest rate, and n is the total number of payments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is amortization?

Amortization is the gradual repayment of a loan over time through scheduled payments.

Does this calculator include compound interest?

Yes. The calculation includes standard compound interest applied monthly.
